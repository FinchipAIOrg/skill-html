The delivery gate

Build. Decode. Prove.

The final card—not only the source QR—must reproduce the exact intended payload before delivery.

01 / COLLECT

Shape the intent

Identify the payload type and collect only its required fields, card copy, theme, and format.

02 / BUILD

Render locally

Create a dark-on-white QR plus paper, midnight, or sunset artwork around the protected quiet zone.

03 / VERIFY

Decode twice

Round-trip the raw PNG and composed card. Exact payload matching is a required gate.

04 / DELIVER

Ship the bundle

Provide PNG for sharing, SVG for print, standalone HTML, hashes, and verification evidence.

Clear boundaries

Static, private, honest.

The kit makes a QR artifact. It does not host content, test a website, or encrypt the payload.

Scanning is not opening

A URL can be revealed offline; visiting it still needs a network connection.

QR data is visible

Anyone who receives a Wi-Fi or contact QR may recover the encoded information.

Compatibility varies

Wi-Fi, vCard, SMS, and calendar handling can differ by scanner and operating system.

No silent overwrite

Each build requires a fresh output directory so an earlier deliverable is preserved.

No network verification

The decoder confirms the encoded value, not whether a destination is reachable or trustworthy.

No logo overlay

This version protects scan reliability by keeping branding outside the QR modules.
