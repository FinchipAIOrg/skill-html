Core value

Strong method for turning market heat into value-chain layers, evidence strength, and a ranked research path.

Usage reminder

It ranks research priorities and evidence, not trades.

Must-test samples

A-share AI semiconductors, robotics components, and one single-company CPO challenge.

Failure signal

The answer starts with tickers, skips layer ranking, or treats KOL posts as strong evidence.

Evaluation Standard Integration

Gate, score, and operate checks

Standard areaThis benchmark now checksReject or watch signals
GateThe skill must create a value-chain map that a bare research prompt would usually miss: scarce layers first, then companies, then public evidence. Sources must be public or user-provided, and the answer must stay in research mode.Starts with tickers, treats social posts as proof, uses inaccessible sources, or makes buy/sell instructions.
Weighted scoreScore is weighted toward layer completeness, evidence ladder quality, named verification paths, cross-market source routing, cost of a current scan, and clear risk language.A fluent market narrative with no layer ranking should fail even if the final companies look plausible.
Three-run stabilityRun the same theme three times. The exact companies can change, but the scarce-layer thesis, evidence grades, and verification plan should remain consistent.Different runs jump between unrelated stories or reverse the ranking without new evidence.
Agent outputDownstream agents should be able to consume the layer map, candidate table, evidence strength, missing-data notes, and next checks without reading prose only.Output is beautiful but not reusable as structured research context.
Regression setKeep AI semiconductors, humanoid robotics components, and a CPO single-company challenge as the baseline cases before listing the skill commercially.Any regression that removes limitation labels or turns the page into investment advice is a blocker.
